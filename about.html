The Passion Is FASHION

FASHION IS MY GREASTEST PASSION.

I enjoy everything that is associated with FASHION. From cosmetics to upscaling. Any chance that I get to use my sewing machine or needle and thread I travel to another world. My designs are inspired by my life and my surroundings. I love having one of a kind pieces. I am currently re-working a dress origanlly designed by Tori Box. I wore this piece for my brother's wedding. I am hoping to send a beautiful young lady to prom in the re-worked piece. I'm keeping my fingers crossed that one day my sister in-law will let me re-design her wedding dress.

Although I love designing, my greatest love when it comes to fashion is MODELING.It doesn't matter if it print, runway, or promotional as long as I'm in front of the camera. I knew i wanted to be a model when I hit the stage for the National American Miss Pageant in 2008. Even though I didn't get crowned everything just felt so natural. Since the pageant I have modeled in several fashions shows. I also attended John Robert Powers, which is a school for aspiring actors, models, and artists. At JRP they taught me how to prepare, present, and audition to stand out from the rest.
